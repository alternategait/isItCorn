Corn and corn product allergies

While it's not common, people can be allergic to corn. This food allergy may cause symptoms such as digestive problems, hives itching, a stuffy nose, or wheezing. Some people with a corn allergy have a serious reaction called anaphylaxis. If you demonstrate signs of anaphylaxis it is a medical emergency and you should call your doctor or local emergency number

This allergen checker is designed to recognize the most common corn product ingredients. However, there may be other corn products or incomplete ingredient data. This site is intended to assist in managing your personalized dietary plan and is not intended as medical advice.
